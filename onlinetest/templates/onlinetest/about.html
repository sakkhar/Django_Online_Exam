About the Developer

This project was conceptualised and designed during the TCS Remote Summer Internship Programme 2017, under the mentorship of Mr. Vaibhav Sharma. The website is designed and maintained by three students from School of Information and Communication Technology. Their details are given below under the Contact Details segment.

The portal helps you take online tests that too free of cost. There is both student login and a client login facility availabe. Online Examination System is a website that can be used for business and training tests, recruitment and pre - employment assessments, health and safety quizzes.

It fulfills the requirements of the organizations/institutes to conduct the exams online. They do not have to go to any software developer to make a separate site for being able to conduct exams online. They just have to register on the site and enter the exam details (exam duration, excel file).

Students can give exam without the need of going to any physical destination. They can view the result at the same time. Administrator has a privilege to create, modify and delete the test papers and its questions. User can register, login and give the test with his specific id. Organizations can view the records of the enrolled students and can analyse the marks of those students who had taken the test. Students get to know their results immediately after submission of the test through a pop-up.

Thus, the purpose of the site is to provide a system that saves the efforts and time of both the institutes and the students and hence conduct online examination in an organised and efficient manner.
